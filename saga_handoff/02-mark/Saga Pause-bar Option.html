How the fold works: in pause mode the two left spines slide together into one solid bar and the two right spines into another — so it’s the same four shapes, just gathered into a two-bar pause instead of spreading into a meter. The triangle⇄glyph morph is identical either way; only the playing end-state differs. The accent carries through: spine #2 (the amber one) folds into the left bar, so the pause reads amber-left / cream-right — The accent that’s a stripe in the meter, a stripe through the play triangle, and the left bar in pause — one consistent accent across every state.

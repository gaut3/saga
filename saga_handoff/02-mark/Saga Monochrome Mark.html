Monochrome mark

A flat, single-colour silhouette of the spine mark for places that can’t take colour: the status bar, notification small icon, and any tinted system surface. No accent bar, no text lines, no gradient — just the shape.

Both are the same silhouette — the resting 4-spine pose, vertically centred. The accent bar is dropped (a monochrome icon can’t carry it) and so are the spine text-lines (they’d disappear or smear at this size).

Android rule: the notification small icon must be white-on-transparent — the OS tints the silhouette to match the system theme, discarding any colour. These are exported white at the five density buckets so they stay crisp from the status bar (24 dp) up.
iOS: use the white master as a template image (renders monochrome, tinted by the system) for things like the Control Center / lock-screen player glyph.

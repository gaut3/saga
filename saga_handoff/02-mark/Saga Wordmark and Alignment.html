Wordmark weight & spine alignment

Two open questions: how heavy should “saga” be (the app reads lighter than extrabold), and should the spines sit on a shelf or grow from the centre? The centre answer also decides how cleanly they fold into the play triangle.

How heavy is “saga”?

01 · MANROPE WEIGHT LADDER
It’s Manrope, but your app is clearly not the 800 I’d been using on the lockups — it reads around 500–600. My pick is 600 (SemiBold): confident and legible at small sizes without the chunky, logo-shouting feel of extrabold. 800 is great only for very large hero moments. (If the app font is actually something other than Manrope, send the name and I’ll match it — but the proportions look like Manrope.)

Centred wins for one concrete reason: a play triangle is vertically symmetric, so centred spines fold straight into it. Bottom-anchored spines fold into a right-angled ramp instead — the morph that ties the brand to the play button only works centred. Centred also pairs better with the wordmark, sitting on the same optical mid-line as the lowercase x-height rather than dropping below the baseline.
